FAQ

How do Web3Forms works?

Web3Forms provides an endpoint for your HTML forms, so that you can start collecting form submission from your website visitors. There is no backend code involved. Only need to put two lines and its compatible with your current tools and technologies.

Is this free plan really free?

Yes, you can use the free plan as long as you needed without even creating an account. Only use your email address to get form submis…

Do you store data submitted through my form?

Form data that you send to the endpoint is forwarded to your email address. We don't store any data from your forms on our server. However we might store the logs in Amazon Web Services for debugging purposes.

Is my email address secure with you?

Yes, we will not share information with any third party. We use your email to send data submitted from the form to your email. However we may contact you to share updates and information about our service.

Why is the Pro plan so cheap?

Web3Forms is developed by enthusiasts who love to change a fair price to cover costs plus a little more for our time. We still don't know why other competitors are charging so high.

I lost my Access Key, what should I do?

Don't worry, just enter your email again to generate a new Access Key and we will send it to you again.

What happens if it exceeds the submission limit?

We don't put any limit on any accounts. However we might send an email to notify you about the same and ask you to upgrade.

How do I cancel my plan?

Just contact us and we will do that for you.
